World's Famous Cofees!

Coffee is a plant (Coffea) and the name of the drink that is made from this plant.
The coffee plant is a bush or tree that can grow up to ten meters (about 32 feet)
high, but is usually cut shorter. Coffee plants originally grew in Africa, and now
also grow in South America, Central America and Southeast Asia and despite
the bans imposed during the 15th century by religious leaders in Mecca and
Cairo, and later by the Catholic Church.
